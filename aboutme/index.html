Robocon

From 2014 to 2017, I was a member of RoboTech, the team for robot contest at The University of Tokyo. In RoboTech, I belonged to mechanic section.

In 2016, I am a team leader of RoboTech. We were winner of National Championships(NHK Robocon) and we participated in the international robot contest “ABU Robocon2016” as Representation from Japan. At this contest, We were 2nd-RunnerUp and we were commened for ABU Robocon award.
